What it does

Everything you manage,
in one place

Five integrated modules that talk to each other — so your life doesn't feel like five separate apps.

📋
Task & calendar

Multi-category task scheduling synced to a localized calendar. Set deadlines, track priorities, and never lose a to-do across contexts.

💰
Financial tracker

Budget constraints, automated recurring bills, and spending overviews — all in real time. Know exactly where your money is going.

📓
Rich journal

Media-rich journal entries backed by Cloud Storage. Capture thoughts, photos, and moments — searchable, always with you.

🏠
Home management

Track household tasks, shopping lists, and maintenance schedules — all linked to the same real-time backend.

🌬️
Wind Down

A custom-animated breathing exercise interface to close your day intentionally. Designed with care for mental wellbeing.

🌗
Light & dark mode

A centralized theming engine with seamless transitions. The app looks great at 7 AM and at midnight.
